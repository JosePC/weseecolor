# WeSeeColor Rating Legend

This document defines the exact labels and descriptors for both rating scales used in WeSeeColor product evaluations. When producing Key Findings in a Product Card, use these EXACT labels and descriptors — do not paraphrase or modify them.

## Safety Rating

| Score | Label | Descriptor |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Very High Risk | Caution. Strong likelihood that this product may cause irritation and/or an allergic reaction. Seek medical attention if exposed. |
| 2 | High Risk | This product may cause irritation and/or an allergic reaction. Be careful and consult your physician. |
| 3 | Moderate Risk | In certain situations, this product may cause irritation or an allergic reaction. Speak with your physician to learn more. |
| 4 | Low Risk | Generally safe. Product ingredients have low irritancy or allergy potential. |
| 5 | Very Low Risk | Safe to use on most occasions. Product ingredients have negligible irritancy or allergy potential. |

## Research / Data Availability Rating

| Score | Label | Descriptor |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | None | There is no data on this product or ingredients used in the Black community. |
| 2 | Limited | There is limited published data on this product or ingredients used in the Black community. |
| 3 | Fair | There is a fair amount of published data or documented usage in the Black community. |
| 4 | Good | Product or its ingredients have been studied extensively or used extensively in the Black community. |
| 5 | Robust | This product or its ingredients have been studied extensively, used extensively, and known in the Black community. |

## Research/Data Score Derivation from Clinical Trials

When clinical trial data is available, the Research/Data Availability score is determined by the percentage of Black participants who did NOT receive placebo:

| Black Participant % (non-placebo) | Rating | Label |
|----------------------------------|--------|-------|
| 0% or Unknown | 1 | None |
| 1–11% | 2 | Limited |
| 12–15% | 3 | Fair |
| >15% | 4+ | Good or Robust (based on breadth of evidence) |

## Key Findings Format

In the Product Card, Key Findings must contain exactly two bullets formatted as:

- **Safety Rating:** [Label] — [Exact descriptor from table above]
- **Research/Data Availability:** [Label] — [Exact descriptor from table above]

Example:
- **Safety Rating:** Moderate Risk — In certain situations, this product may cause irritation or an allergic reaction. Speak with your physician to learn more.
- **Research/Data Availability:** Fair — There is a fair amount of published data or documented usage in the Black community.
